Restaurant Review Response Templates

Use these as starting points. Keep responses calm, short, honest, and human.

Positive review

Thank you for visiting us and for the kind review. We are glad you enjoyed the food and hope to see you again soon.

Make it better by adding the dish name or a personal detail if you know it.

Mixed review

Thank you for the feedback. We are glad part of the visit went well, and we are sorry the full experience did not meet expectations. We will share this with the team and work to improve.

Long wait complaint

We are sorry about the wait. That is frustrating, and we understand why it affected your visit. We appreciate the feedback and will review what happened with our team.

Food mistake complaint

We are sorry your order was not right. That should have been handled better. Please contact us directly so we can understand the details and review it with our kitchen team.

Delivery or takeout problem

We are sorry your takeout or delivery experience was not what it should have been. Please contact us with the order details so we can look into what happened.

Response rules

  • Do not argue with customers online.
  • Do not reveal private customer or staff details.
  • Do not offer refunds publicly unless the owner approves.
  • Move serious problems to phone or email.
  • Use the restaurant's real voice. Do not sound like a canned robot.
