Privacy Policy

RestaurantAIBot is built to help visitors search for restaurants while keeping privacy language clear and simple.

Information you choose to provide

If you email RestaurantAIBot, we may receive your email address and anything you include in your message, such as a restaurant name, city, website, or question.

Restaurant searches

Search text may be used to understand what food, city, dining style, or budget you are looking for. Search activity may also be reviewed to improve the website and make results more useful.

Location access

Location access is optional. If you allow it, your browser may share approximate location coordinates so RestaurantAIBot can search nearby restaurants. You can deny location access and search by city instead.

Third-party services

The site may use third-party services such as AI tools, maps, and restaurant data providers. These services may process search requests or map-related data to return results.

What we do not do

  • We do not ask for payment information on this site.
  • We do not commit private API keys to the public repository.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not guarantee restaurant hours, menus, prices, allergy details, or availability.
