Today’s feature: Smart Promo Cards

A Smart Promo Card is a small, clearly labeled restaurant offer that can appear inside search results, city pages, cuisine pages, or travel pages when it fits the visitor’s food search.

  • Best for lunch specials, dinner offers, slow nights, happy hour, and local events.
  • Can match by city, cuisine, meal time, local/traveler mode, and search intent.
  • Does not block the visitor. It should feel helpful, not annoying.
  • Paid versions will require review, disclosure, and payment setup before launch.
