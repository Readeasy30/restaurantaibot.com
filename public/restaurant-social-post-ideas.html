Restaurant Social Post Ideas

Simple post ideas that help local restaurants stay visible without sounding fake or complicated.

10 easy posts to rotate

1. Today's special

Show the dish, price if approved, and when it is available.

2. Best-selling dish

Post one strong photo and explain why customers like it.

3. Behind the scenes

Show prep, baking, sauce-making, staff setup, or fresh ingredients.

4. Meet the team

Introduce an owner, cook, server, or family member.

5. Review reply

Thank a customer and mention the dish or experience they enjoyed.

6. Menu reminder

Help customers find the menu link and know what to order.

7. Takeout reminder

Show the pickup process and best takeout items.

8. Catering or parties

Explain what events the restaurant can help with.

9. Local event tie-in

Connect with a nearby game, show, festival, or weekend crowd.

10. Weekend hours

Post hours before people start making plans.

Simple weekly pattern

  • Monday: quiet-night or lunch idea
  • Tuesday: featured dish
  • Wednesday: behind-the-scenes post
  • Thursday: weekend reminder
  • Friday: best-seller or event post
  • Saturday/Sunday: hours, brunch, family meal, or takeout reminder

Posting rule

A good restaurant post should answer one question: why should someone visit, order, call, reserve, or remember this restaurant today?
