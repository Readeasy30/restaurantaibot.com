Local Restaurant SEO Basics

Local search starts with clear facts, useful pages, and steady updates.

What local SEO means

For a restaurant, local SEO means making it easier for nearby customers to understand what you serve, where you are, when you are open, and why they should choose you.

Core basics

  • Use the same name, address, and phone number across the website and major profiles.
  • Keep hours and holiday hours current.
  • Make the menu easy to read on a phone.
  • Create clear pages for catering, private events, brunch, takeout, delivery, or outdoor dining when offered.
  • Add real photos that match the restaurant today.

Useful local content

  • Best dishes for lunch nearby.
  • Family dinner options in the neighborhood.
  • Private party or catering information.
  • Local event meal ideas.
  • Seasonal specials and updated hours.

Simple rule

Every page should help a customer decide faster. Clear beats clever.
