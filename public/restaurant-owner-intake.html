Owner photo and fact rule

Real customer websites should use owner-supplied facts and owner-approved photos only. Do not invent hours, menu items, prices, reviews, awards, photos, allergy claims, or sales guarantees.

What happens next

  1. Restaurant owner sends the details by email.
  2. RestaurantAIBot reviews the restaurant, offer, and requested service manually.
  3. If details are missing, we send one short missing-details list.
  4. If it is a fit, we prepare a founder card, promo idea, or website starter draft.
  5. The owner reviews one preview link and sends one combined correction list.
  6. Nothing paid or live is activated without confirmation.
