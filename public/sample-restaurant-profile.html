What a customer site can show

Menu highlights

Owner-supplied dishes such as tacos, bowls, family meals, desserts, drinks, or specials.

Hours and location

Clear address, neighborhood, hours, phone number, and directions link.

Ordering and reservations

Links to the restaurant’s existing ordering, delivery, reservation, or social page.

Local search text

Plain text that helps customers and crawlers understand the restaurant, city, cuisine, and best uses.

Repeatable build path

  1. Owner sends restaurant facts through the intake page.
  2. RestaurantAIBot checks for missing details.
  3. The site is generated from the standard restaurant website template.
  4. Files are stored in GitHub and published by Cloudflare Pages.
  5. The owner reviews one live preview link and sends one combined correction list.

Important status

This is a sample demo only. Real customer websites must use confirmed owner-supplied facts and owner-approved photos. RestaurantAIBot does not invent hours, menu items, photos, prices, reviews, awards, or guarantees.
