Restaurant Catering and Private Events Page Tips

A clear catering or event page can help customers ask better questions and plan faster.

Who this page helps

  • Restaurants with party trays or group meals.
  • Restaurants with private rooms or event space.
  • Cafes and diners that serve local business lunches.
  • Food trucks that book events.
  • Family restaurants that handle birthdays, teams, clubs, or office meals.

What to include

  • Group size range.
  • Food options or sample trays.
  • Pickup, delivery, dine-in, or private-room details.
  • How much notice is needed.
  • Photos of catering trays, event tables, or group meals.
  • Phone number or simple inquiry email.

Simple page copy example

Planning lunch for the office, a family party, or a local event? Contact us about group meals, party trays, and private dining options. Tell us the date, guest count, pickup or dine-in preference, and the food style you want.

Questions to answer

  • How many people can you serve?
  • Do you need advance notice?
  • Can customers pick up?
  • Do you deliver locally?
  • Is there a room charge or minimum?
  • Who should customers contact?

Simple rule

If catering or events can bring profit, do not hide that service on a hard-to-find page.
