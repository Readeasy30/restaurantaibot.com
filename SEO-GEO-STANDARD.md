# SEO and AI Search Standard

Use this repo standard for RestaurantAIBot pages.

Every important page should include:

- One clear topic.
- A direct answer near the top.
- Helpful headings.
- Internal links to related pages.
- A sitemap entry.
- An llms.txt entry when the page is important.

Keep the site plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Do not redesign or change architecture unless Gerry asks.
