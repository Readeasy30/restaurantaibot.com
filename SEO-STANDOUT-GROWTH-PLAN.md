# SEO + Standout Feature Growth Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-14

## Purpose

Make RestaurantAIBot easier to find in search engines and more memorable for visitors.

This plan follows the safe build direction:

- helpful people-first content
- clear site structure
- crawlable pages
- honest restaurant disclaimers
- useful internal links
- no keyword stuffing
- no fake reviews
- no fake restaurant listings
- no intrusive ads
- no live paid placements until review/payment/legal rules are ready

## Content rules

Every SEO page should answer:

1. Who is this page for?
2. What problem does it solve?
3. What can the visitor search for?
4. What should the visitor confirm before acting?
5. What page should they visit next?

Use simple wording. Aim for grade 7 to 9 reading level.

Avoid:

- fake rankings
- fake reviews
- fake restaurant availability
- copied content from competitors
- keyword-stuffed city pages
- claiming restaurants are open unless live data confirms it
- claiming allergy safety
- claiming full translation before it exists
