# Customer site review checklist

Last updated: 2026-06-18

Use this checklist before sending any restaurant customer website preview.

## Business facts

- Restaurant name is correct.
- Owner/contact name is correct if shown.
- Phone number is correct.
- Address is correct.
- City and neighborhood are correct.
- Cuisine is correct.
- Hours are correct or clearly marked as owner-provided.
- Menu highlights match owner-supplied details.
- Offer wording matches owner-supplied details.

## Links

- Call button works.
- Directions link works.
- Ordering link works if supplied.
- Reservation link works if supplied.
- Social links work if supplied.
- Contact email link works if supplied.
- Navigation links work.

## Pages

- Homepage exists.
- Menu page exists when part of the package.
- Catering/private events page exists when part of the package.
- Gallery page exists when part of the package.
- Contact page exists.
- Privacy page exists.
- Terms page exists.
- Sitemap exists.
- robots.txt exists.
- llms.txt exists.

## Mobile check

- Homepage is readable on phone width.
- Buttons are easy to tap.
- Menu sections are easy to scan.
- Address and phone are easy to find.
- No text runs off screen.
- No images break the layout.

## SEO and crawler check

- Page title includes restaurant name, city, and cuisine.
- Meta description is plain and accurate.
- H1 is clear.
- Important text is crawlable, not hidden only in JavaScript.
- FAQ or question-answer section is present.
- Sitemap lists public pages.
- llms.txt describes the site.

## Safety check

- No invented reviews.
- No invented awards.
- No invented photos.
- No invented menu prices.
- No invented health, allergy, or nutrition claims.
- No ranking, traffic, order, or sales guarantees.
- No private customer information exposed.
- No card data collected on the customer site.

## Owner approval

Before final launch, confirm:

- Owner approved restaurant facts.
- Owner approved photos or placeholders.
- Owner approved phone and address.
- Owner approved menu highlights.
- Owner approved ordering/reservation links.
- Owner sent one combined correction list or approved as-is.

## Lock rule

After owner approval, update the customer `STATUS.md` file with:

- Date approved.
- Pages confirmed.
- Known open items.
- Next monthly update window.
