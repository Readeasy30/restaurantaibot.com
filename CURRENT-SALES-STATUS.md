## Operating model

1. Owner sends details.
2. Details are reviewed.
3. Site is generated from a standard template.
4. Files are committed to GitHub.
5. Cloudflare Pages publishes the site.
6. Owner reviews the link.
7. One correction list is handled.
8. Final version is locked.

## Important limits

- No private keys in GitHub.
- No customer card data collected on RestaurantAIBot.com.
- No live owner dashboard yet.
- No upload system yet.
- No custom online ordering system.
- Use owner-provided facts and photos only.
