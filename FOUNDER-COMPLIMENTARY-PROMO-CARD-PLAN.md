# Founder Complimentary Promo Card Plan

Last updated: 2026-06-14

## Purpose

Use complimentary restaurant promo cards as a short launch bridge until paid checkout and final sponsored-placement rules are ready.

This is not a live paid ad system.

This is a manual-review founder program designed to:

- seed the site with real restaurant examples
- build relationships with restaurant owners
- test which offers work
- create screenshots and proof for sales
- help early restaurants before the paid system is active
- make conversion to paid Smart Promo Cards easier later

## Name

Use this public name:

> Founder Complimentary Promo Card

Do not call it a free ad network.
Do not promise traffic, sales, ranking, or guaranteed placement.

## Public offer

Suggested wording:

> We are offering a small number of complimentary Founder Promo Cards while RestaurantAIBot finishes paid setup. Selected restaurants may receive a clearly labeled sample promo card at no cost for a short launch period.

## Eligibility

Good candidates:

- real local restaurants
- restaurants with clear contact information
- restaurants with a real address or verified service area
- restaurants with a simple offer or clear menu highlight
- restaurants willing to confirm details before publication
- restaurants that may later want paid promo cards or a better website

Avoid:

- unclear ownership
- broken websites
- fake discounts
- unverifiable businesses
- misleading urgency
- allergy or health claims
- illegal offers
- copied brand names
- restaurants that do not want public contact

## Recommended free period

Keep it short and clean.

Recommended options:

- 7 days complimentary
- 14 days complimentary
- Founder batch ending on a specific date

Best first version:

> Complimentary for the first 14 days after manual approval.

After the free period:

- remove the card
- continue as unpaid profile only
- invite owner to upgrade to paid Smart Promo Card
- offer Website Starter if their site is weak

## Limits

Limit the first batch so it stays manageable.

Recommended first batch:

- 10 restaurants max
- 1 promo card per restaurant
- 1 city/cuisine/meal-time target per restaurant
- no automatic renewal
- no guaranteed clicks
- no guaranteed ranking
- no live payment collection yet

## Required label

All cards must be clearly labeled.

Use:

> Founder complimentary feature

Disclosure:

> Complimentary launch feature. Not a paid placement. Restaurant details must be confirmed before visiting.

When paid placement starts later, use:

> Sponsored local offer

Never mix the labels.

## Owner intake fields

Collect:

- restaurant name
- owner/contact name
- owner email
- phone
- website
- address
- city
- neighborhood
- cuisine
- offer headline
- offer details
- best days and times
- target customer: locals, travelers, families, date night, lunch crowd, late night
- preferred target area
- existing ordering or reservation link
- confirmation that the restaurant approves the public card

## Card content rules

A founder card should include:

- restaurant name
- cuisine
- city/neighborhood
- short offer headline
- short offer text
- why it may appear
- call-to-action link
- clear complimentary disclosure

Avoid:

- countdowns
- fake scarcity
- guaranteed savings
- medical/allergy claims
- best-in-city claims
- review claims unless verified

## Manual workflow

1. Restaurant submits inquiry.
2. Gerry reviews restaurant and offer.
3. Confirm details by email.
4. Add card to demo/founder promo data.
5. Mark card as founder complimentary.
6. Show only in suitable search contexts.
7. Review after 7 to 14 days.
8. Offer paid continuation or Website Starter.

## Conversion path

After the complimentary period, send owner a simple message:

> Your complimentary Founder Promo Card period is ending. We can remove it, keep only a basic profile, or continue it as a paid Smart Promo Card when checkout is ready.

Suggested paid upgrade:

- Smart Promo Card: $49/month
- Slow Night Push: $79/month
- Traveler Visibility: $99/month
- Restaurant Website Starter: $299 setup + $39/month

## Why this is useful

This is a good move because it lets RestaurantAIBot:

- test the promo-card idea with real businesses
- collect owner feedback
- build screenshots for future sales
- avoid rushing payment setup
- create goodwill with early restaurants
- show visitors what a useful restaurant offer looks like

## Safety locks

Do not add:

- live payment checkout
- hidden tracking
- automatic approval
- owner dashboard
- user accounts
- auction/bidding
- guaranteed results claims
- full-screen popups
- fake reviews
- fake discounts

## Decision

Use complimentary cards as a temporary, manual-review launch tool.

The offer should be generous but controlled:

> A small number of manually reviewed Founder Complimentary Promo Cards while paid setup is being finished.
